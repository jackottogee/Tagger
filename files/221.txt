
TypeScript Developer

Design and code high-quality applications using TypeScript. 
Develop front-end web applications using TypeScript and related technologies. 
Integrate TypeScript applications into existing systems and databases. 
Test and debug applications to ensure they perform as expected. 
Collaborate with other developers, designers, and product managers to ensure successful execution of projects. 
Contribute to the development of best practices and coding standards. 
Stay up to date with the latest technologies and trends in the industry. 

Requirements: 

Bachelor’s degree in computer science or related field. 
Minimum of 3 years’ experience with TypeScript development. 
Proficiency with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. 
Experience with popular JavaScript frameworks such as Angular and React. 
Knowledge of web performance optimization techniques. 
Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail. 
Excellent written and verbal communication skills. 

Benefits: 

Competitive salary and bonus structure.

Paid vacation and sick days. 
Flexible work hours. 
Opportunities for career growth and development.
