About Us
Toolagen is a specialist software services company based in Redhill, Surrey with a focus on building bespoke products. We work with enterprise customers and also SaaS Startups.
Job Description
As a Business Development Manager, you will be responsible for:
· Good knowledge of the software services industry
· Creating and managing a pipeline of net new business opportunities
· Growing the revenue base across the software portfolio via new business sales, cross selling and upselling
· Exceeding sales revenue targets and other critical success metrics
· Building business-class relationships and nurturing trust with key decision-makers
· Qualifying opportunities in terms of chance of success, competition, timescales, procurement process and win criteria
· Identifying opportunities for campaigns, services, and distribution channels that will lead to incremental sales.
· Using your knowledge of the market and competitors, to identify and develop the company's unique selling propositions and differentiators
· Meet clients face to face as needed to close the sale
· Attend exhibitions, fairs and networking events to find new business
The Successful Applicant
As a Business Development Manager, you will have:
· A proven track record of success within a consulting sales or software sales
· Experience in a New Business role selling technology services into the enterprise or SMB space
· An understanding of how and why customers buy software services
· Experience hitting or exceeding challenging sales targets
· Excellent communication and presentation skills, with the ability to expertly demonstrate a range of software solutions.
· An ability to solve problems and act on own initiative
· Previously worked with a Microsoft Partner in growing their service practices
