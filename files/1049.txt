An exciting opportunity for an experienced Product Developer/Technologist interested in home fixtures and fittings to join our established product design team.
You will be involved in all technical aspects of the product development process, proactively evolving current products and developing new ideas from initial design to final production.
Main responsibilities:

Taking new ideas and helping develop them into beautiful and functional products from prototype to production.
Evolving and improving current products
Managing the development process critical pathway within the team and across the business.
Liaising with our manufacturers and suppliers, developing good relationships, managing the development, cost negotiations, lead times, and MOQs
Submitting development specifications to factories and providing technical support
Ensuring all samples received from the manufacturers are on time and as expected
Finalising and getting sign-off on all samples before bulk order placement
Researching relevant materials and processes with an eye on innovative solutions
Keeping up to date with the latest home interior developments and ensuring our products stay current or ahead of the market.
Ensuring product quality and aesthetics are fit for our market and brand.

You must be:

Able to work independently and collaboratively
Proficient in SOLIDWORKS
Skilled in producing prototypes and performing functionality tests
Familiar and experienced in product testing, certifications and compliance
Knowledgeable in manufacturing techniques and processes
Comfortable making samples, e.g. using laser cutters and 3D printers
Willing to travel occasionally to production facilities in China, India and Europe
Keen to be involved, with a hands-on approach
Solution-focused.
