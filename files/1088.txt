About DarkInvader
DarkInvader is a UK-based cyber security provider focussing on dark web and threat intelligence through its SaaS platform and highly-skilled team of security researchers.
Our goal is to provide our customers with peace of mind by providing the UK’s leading cyber threat intelligence solution. Our mission is to passionately innovate new approaches to cyber threat intelligence processing.
Role InfoWe are looking for a passionate and dedicated developer who would like to be a part of a fast growing team and work on a highly innovative product.
We use TDD for the development process and a mixture of GitLab CI/CD + AWS services to deploy our code into production.We believe that the code can be deployed as often as required without planning inconvenient maintenance windows.
The production environments are set up with AWS ECS (Fargate) and even if you do not know much about, do not worry! We will provide all necessary training :)
