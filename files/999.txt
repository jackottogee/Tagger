 CITI is one of the largest players in the financial industry, employing more than 200,000 people across the globe.

 What we do? (What the team does) 
Our team (XiP) is responsible for supplying both backend and frontend services to Citi trading desks and enterprise users in the largest global financial markets and exchanges in New York, London and other major financial hubs.

 What will you do?

 Role is for an experienced Python Software Engineer to aid the XiP team in leading the development of client-facing libraries and SDKs for XiP's APIs.
 It will involve collaboration with the XiP team and working with senior end users in the business and technology on developing reusable libraries, validating solutions, and diagnosing and resolving issues.
 The role requires experience of developing software using REST and providing fault-tolerant client-server solutions.
 The candidate must have familiarity with standard and networking python libraries, testing frameworks (Pytest), analysis tools and a good understanding of CI/CD practices.
 Some experience of Java is plus, to aid understanding of Java-based API service implementations.


 What you will bring 

Solid core Python 3.6+ programming of object-oriented applications
 Demonstrable ability to leverage best coding practices: clean and readable code, automated testing, code reviews, continuous integration and delivery
 Demonstrable experience in writing reusable, testable and efficient code with proper error and exception handling
 Demonstrable experience in writing high quality unit and integration tests, strong debugging skills
 Experience of test-driven development and of continuous integration platforms.
 Hands-on experience in handling various data structures, for example CSV, JSON, XML, etc.
 Understanding of Agile software development approach and experience in using source control (preferably git)
 Strong object-oriented design skills and experience in identifying and applying design patterns
