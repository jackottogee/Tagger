 About the Role
   As a Java full stack developer in designing, implementing, and deploying as part of a seasoned production team.
   For systems analysis, solution design, front and back-end development and continuous deployment within an AWS cloud environment.
 

 Responsibilities
 

 Writing and reviewing portions of detailed specifications for the development of system components of moderate complexity ensuring continues improvement.
 Collaborating with development teams to understand product requirements and translate them into innovative software designs.
 Implementing development processes, coding best practices, and code reviews, keeping up to date with new technology
 Operating in an Agile environment to resolve technical issues and complete bug fixes
 Collaborating with teams and stakeholders to seek feedback on priorities to help shape innovative solutions for complicated software challenges.


 Requirements
 

 Demonstrate good experience as a Software Engineer and knowledge of software development methodologies (e.g., Agile, Waterfall
 Have proficiency in development languages such as frameworks in Java/J2EE, Spring / Restful web services, SQL. And experience in (JavaScript/Angular/Reacts)
 Be familiar with industry best practices including code coverage with Junit test
 Experience with engineering best practices such as TDD, Pair Programming, Continuous Integration, automated testing, and deployment
 Experience in AWS development and deployment experience, including specific knowledge of Kubernetes
 Have great verbal and written communication skills and enjoy collaborating with teams to solve problems
 Have the ability to write and review detailed specifications for the development of complex system components
 Have experience of test-driven development and the ability to complete complex bug fixes
