Who are we looking for?
We are looking for a hands-on Software Development Manager who would plan, direct, and coordinates all activities related to delivering software programs. You should be familiar with all the policies, procedures and technical issues related to software programming. The software development manager is the “go to” person when it comes to software developers. They are also in charge of managing the activities of the software development team.
Who are we?
We are a team of industry experts committed to empowering telcos to digitally transform their brands. Through our carefully designed software solutions, we enable our clients to create highly engaging customer experiences, digitalise their operations and accelerate business growth.
