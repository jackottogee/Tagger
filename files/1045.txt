Role overview
We are looking for an experienced Developer with SQL and JSON experience to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in SQL database development, Transact SQL & JSON, with experience in designing, implementing, and maintaining databases. In this role, you will be responsible for developing, testing, and optimizing SQL queries, as well as designing and implementing database schema changes.
