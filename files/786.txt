
Introduction


We are looking for a Software Engineer to tackle complex and challenging problems and deliver cost effective, reliable, supportable solutions by using modern software engineering methods and cutting-edge techniques. You will have the opportunity to work across the entire Software Lifecycle and have the responsibility of developing and deploying web-based applications to a number of systems across the defence space.
 My client does offer some flexibility on remote working, however due to the level of security clearance required you will need to be based on-site in their Cheltenham office for the majority of work.

Due to the nature of the work, candidates must hold an active DV Clearance or be willing to undergo this clearance.

Perform demonstrations to internal and external customers of development work
