
Introduction


We are looking for a Software Engineer to work across a range of technologies and provide support to solutions for MOD and NATO systems. My client is open to Junior candidates or candidates who have recently finished a Grad Scheme, as long as you have a few key skills that are mentioned in the description below.
 This is a remote working role, however on-site work in my clients Southwest London office will be required once or twice a month.
 My client are a leading global defence organisation who supply their customers with world class software engineering solutions for a number of complex systems. Candidates must be eligible to hold a SC Security Clearance to be considered for this opportunity.

Design, build, deliver and support critical customer systems

Skills & experience



Experience in design, development, test and integration of software.
Experience in one of the following: Java, C or C++.

Knowledge or experience in software configuration management, such as Git/BitBucket.
Keen to learn a broad range of technologies and languages.
